7. Maharatrian Usal

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cup mixed sprouts
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic
  • 2 medium onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 handful coriander leaves

Instruction

Step 1
Gather the ingredients.
Step 2
Saute the cumin seeds To prepare this easy breakfast recipe, take oil in a wok/kadhai and heat it. When the oil is hit enough, add cumin seeds & let them splutter.
Step 3
Fry the onions Add the sliced onions, minced ginger and garlic in the kadhai. Saute till the onions become transparent on a medium flame.
Step 4
 Prepare the masala for usal Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, chilly powder, goda masala or garam masala and fry again till the tomatoes are cooked & oil starts leaving the sides.
Step 5
Cook sprouts in the masala Add the cooked sprouts along with the water, if any, along with salt & let them cook together on a slow flame for 4 to 5 minutes. If the mixture is too dry add a little water when adding the sprouts to get it to a gravy consistency. Garnish it with coriander leaves. Serve hot.
