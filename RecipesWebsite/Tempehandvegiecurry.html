3. Tempeh and vegie curry

Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 300g packet tempeh, cut into 1.5cm cubes
  • 1 large red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp Korma curry paste
  • 270ml can lite coconut milk
  • 500g butternut pumpkin, peeled, seeded, diced
  • 250g broccoli, trimmed, cut into florets
  • 150g green beans, trimmed, sliced
  • 200g grape tomatoes, halved
  • Coriander sprigs, to serve
  • Steamed basmati rice, to serve

Instruction

Step 1
Gather the ingredients.
Step 2
Heat half the olive oil in a large saucepan over high heat. Cook tempeh, stirring occasionally, Heat remaining oil in same pan over medium-high heat. Cook onion, stirring occasionally, for 3-4 minutes or until softened. Add curry paste and cook, stirring, for 1 minute or until fragrant. Add coconut milk, pumpkin and 1 cup water. Cover, and simmer for 5 minutes.
Step 3
Add broccoli, beans and tomatoes. Simmer, covered, for 3-4 minutes or until vegetables are just tender. Serve curry with steamed rice, garnished with coriander.
