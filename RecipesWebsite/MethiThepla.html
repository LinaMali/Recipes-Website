8.Methi Thepla

Ingredients
  • 1 cup Wheat Flour
  • 2 crushed Green Chillies
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Haldi (turmeric)
  • 1/4 cup Curd
  • 1 tbsp Ginger & Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Ajwain 1 pinch Hing
  • 1/2 bunch Fresh Methi Leaves
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil as needed

Instruction

Step 1
Gather the ingredients.
Step 2
Put wheat flour in a flat vessel bowl.
Step 3
Mix all the ingredients int it including the methi leaves.
Step 4
Add 1 tsp oil and knead it. Let it stay for 15 minutes.
Step 5
Then make even size small balls of the dough.
Step 6
Roll them separately with a rolling pin into a round shape. br> Step 7
Heat the griddle and roast each of the rolled out theplas one byone. br> Step 8
First roast one side,turn it upside down. br> Step 9
Then put 1/2 tsp oil on the turned side and again it upside down. br> Step 10
Repeat the same with the other side. br> Step 11
When evenly roasted remove it from griddle. br> Step 12
Repeat this for all the balls. br> Step 13
Serve the gujarati methi thepla hot with chundoo or chutney or curd.
